Riverside views in Da Nang

Our 28-story hotel overlooks the Han River, next to the iconic Dragon Bridge and five minutes from Da Nang beaches. Our 24-hour fitness center features yoga and stretching space, with more room for relaxation on the fourth-floor pool terrace. Enjoy all-day dining in our restaurant and café, and cocktails with panoramic views from The Sail lounge.
